Capstone One Proposal

CalculTube

For any high school calculus topic, users can nominate and vote on YouTube videos that they think best explains the topic. (Think of it like Reddit)

  • List of topics are manually added based on the AP calculus standards (BC-only topics are tagged accordingly).
  • Can have different categories to vote on: best basics, best in depth, and funniest.

Structure

Database Tables

  • users
    • one-to-many with videos for nomination
    • many-to-many with videos for favorites
  • topics
    • many-to-many with videos for relevance
  • videos
  • favorites
    • secondary between users and videos
  • topics-videos
    • secondary between topics and videos

Pages

  • An about page for how the site functions. It also contain resources such as links to practice problems.
  • Various user forms and pages.
  • A page for each topic with info from the College Board website. Nominated videos' title show up here for votes and reports. Video title links to a page for the video
  • Each video has a page with the video and details about the video. User can vote on the page and report irrelevant videos.

Misc

Use Flask-Admin for moderation and initial setup for topics.
